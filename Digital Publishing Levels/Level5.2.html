You just don’t matter, Narrator.
To quote your poster,


                                                                                                                                                                         “Making money is turning solitude into a diary. In other words, it’s shit.”
.
A golden bird in my heart dictated these prophetic words.                                                                         I read them before you in this conference of absent writers of New York.
.
This is all bullshit.
And I denounce it.
.
I won’t be taken for a fool. I’ve been playing Mariquita Samper for too long.
.
I quit! I’m leaving The Intimate Diary of
Solitude forever.
.
